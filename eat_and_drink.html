Best Places to Eat & Drink in Dubai

Al Mahara

Located in Burj Al Arab, this restaurant offers an underwater dining experience with fresh seafood.

Pierchic

Set at the end of a pier with stunning views, Pierchic serves Mediterranean seafood in a romantic setting.

La Petite Maison

Enjoy fine French-Mediterranean cuisine at La Petite Maison, known for its light and fresh dishes.

Zuma

A chic Japanese izakaya-style restaurant in DIFC, Zuma offers modern Japanese dishes with a twist.

At.mosphere

Dine on the 122nd floor of the Burj Khalifa and enjoy breathtaking views alongside gourmet international cuisine.
